Crystal Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking the crystal you will add a certain amount of points to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You will lose if you go over the score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
